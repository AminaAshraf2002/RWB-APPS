Web portal design company in Dubai

Tomsher is a premium web portal development company in Dubai, providing B2B & B2C web portal development services. B2B (business-to-business) portal is the easiest way to promote products or services to corporate and companies. B2B portals serve as a digital marketplace where businesses can buy and sell products or services, manage orders, and communicate with their partners, suppliers, and distributors. B2B portals typically display a comprehensive catalog of products or services offered by the participating businesses. This allows potential buyers to browse and search for items they need. B2B portals often include RFQ functionality, enabling buyers to request quotes from multiple suppliers for specific products or services.

B2C portals, also known as Business-to-Consumer portals, are the products/services online store for individual consumers. B2C portals typically showcase a wide range of products or services with detailed descriptions, images, pricing information, and bundle offers. B2C portals often offer the option for users to create accounts, which can provide benefits like order tracking, personalized recommendations, and easier future purchases. B2C companies know that if customers are not attracted or purchased during the first visit to the website, they may lose that potential customer. In B2C websites, selling straight-to-consumer often happens within one website browsing session. The product purchase happens after the customer checked multiple other sites, reviews, and ratings, and compares prices, and brands.

WordPress Website Designer & Page Builders

WordPress is a web-based content management system that enables the creation and publication of content on the Internet. A content management system (CMS) is a software solution that facilitates the creation, editing, organization, and publication of content. Features of WordPress include integrated link management, search engine friendly, clean permalink structure, ability to assign multiple categories to posts, and support for tagging the posts. Utilizing WordPress plugins is an effective approach to enhancing website functionality and effortlessly incorporating extra features. Elementor, WP Page Builder, Beaver Builder, SiteOrigin Page Builder, Gutenberg by Kadence Blocks, Themify Builder, Live Composer, Page Builder Sandwich, and Fusion Page Builder are among the free WordPress page builders.
