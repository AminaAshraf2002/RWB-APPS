Ecwid experts in Dubai

Ecwid (stands for ECommerce WIDget) is a hosted ecommerce platform. Tomsher provides a customized Ecwid store, which is suitable for small business owners to create an ecommerce website and sell their products online. Ecwid online storefront can embed it on other platforms via plug-ins. In the Ecwid platform, free and premium plans (Venture, Business, and Unlimited) are available. The premium plan selection is based on the features and functionalities of your online store and business.

In the Ecwid platform, you can sell physical/ digital products and services. On the free plan, the store can include up to 5 items, but the paid plan provides more product listing and features. Ecwid platform allows customers to add an online store to an existing website. Also, the paid plan has the facility of listing products on Facebook shop, Instagram store, Amazon and eBay.

Start Online Business with Ecwid Platform

Ecwid is a great option for users who want a simple, elegant, and fast-to-develop online store.

Online store and website

Set up your Ecwid online store with their free or paid plans. This platform is recommending that startup companies or individuals develop an ecommerce website with minimum cost.

Connect & Integrate

Build a new online store and connect with the physical store POS. The ecommerce site can easily connect to social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. Also can integrate with online marketplaces like Amazon, eBay, Walmart and Google Shopping.

Tracking & Offers

The ecommerce store can integrate with third-party codes likes Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc. for tracking customer behaviors. This platform included automated emails for marketing, abandoned cart, etc. The store owner can activate discount coupons and gift cards.

Multilingual & Payment
Integration

The store can integrate multiple payment gateways and shipping options for better user selection. The Business and Unlimited plan provides 53 different languages for translation. This multilingual website has the advantage of selling products in different countries.
