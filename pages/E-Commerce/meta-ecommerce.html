How Meta Commerce Manager Supports Online Sales

Meta Commerce Manager is a powerful solution developed by Facebook and Instagram to help businesses organize and control their ecommerce operations in one place. It enables merchants to upload product catalogs, launch a digital storefront, track orders, and activate checkout options if eligible—all within Meta’s connected platforms.

With tools like Facebook Shops and Instagram Shopping, brands can create seamless shopping experiences directly from their profiles. Product catalogs showcase inventory visually, while Facebook Marketplace helps nearby customers find trusted products from local or branded sellers.
