Ecommerce Store development in PrestaShop

PrestaShop is a freemium open source e-commerce platform. We help you to develop your ecommerce store with specific features and everything for your business/brand to grow online. The online store enables you to sell products, accept online payments, manage deliveries and keep track the inventory. The themes & modules can customized based on the requirements and the website backend of the PrestaShop is userfriendly.

PrestaShop is one of the top platform specialized for online store, with the help of web experts to development and management the ecommerce sites. This platform included free themes but additional costs for the installation of addons(paid themes, templates, modules, functionalities). If you reduce the addons & images, the platform speed will increase. This platform support 75 languages and different currencies.
