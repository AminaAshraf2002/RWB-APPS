SMS Marketing FAQs

What is sms marketing?

What is SMS marketing? SMS marketing is a direct marketing method used to send offers, alerts, OTPs, or updates to users via mobile text. It’s fast, personal, and delivers high engagement by reaching users directly on their phones.

What is Bulk sms marketing?

Bulk SMS marketing lets businesses send a large number of messages to selected customers, helping drive sales, promote services, and improve customer engagement through mass communication.

What is the difference between transactional SMS and promotional SMS?

Promotional SMS sends deals and product ads to potential customers. Transactional SMS shares critical updates like order info, OTPs, or service alerts triggered by user activity.
