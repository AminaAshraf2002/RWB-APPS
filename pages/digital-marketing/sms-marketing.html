SMS Marketing FAQs

What is sms marketing?

Short message service (SMS) marketing is a direct marketing strategy that involves sending promotional messages, offers, updates or otp to customers via text messages on their mobile phones. It allows businesses to reach their audience instantly, offering a high engagement rate due to the immediacy and personal nature of SMS.

What is Bulk sms marketing?

Bulk SMS marketing is the process of sending large volumes of promotional or informational text messages to a targeted group of customers to drive engagement, sales, or brand awareness.

What is the difference between transactional SMS and promotional SMS?

Promotional SMS marketing focuses on sending marketing messages to promote products or offers, while transactional SMS delivers essential, action-triggered updates like order confirmations or account alerts.
